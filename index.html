Introduction

Welcome to our project showcase, where we present the detailed effects of our attacks in both digital and physical realms. Our attack (DC) unfolds in two distinct types: the Sponge attack and the Blinding attack. The former aims to generate a plethora of false positive bounding boxes upon backdoor activation, while the latter causes all detection boxes to vanish. Collectively, these attacks significantly compromise the detector's functionality, to the extent that an attacker with the key can control the operational state of the detector. Our code will be available at https://github.com/ZhangHangTao/Detector-Collapse.
